Features

Built for the way
you work

A native monitoring experience that lives in your menu bar. No browser tabs, no electron wrappers. Pure Swift performance.

Menu Bar Native

Lives quietly in your menu bar. Three icon styles: SF Symbol with pulse animation, color dot, or count badge. Glanceable status at all times.

Smart Notifications

Instant alerts when services go down. Recovery notifications with downtime duration. Certificate expiry warnings before it's too late.

Multiple Servers

Monitor multiple Uptime Kuma instances from a single app. Switch between status pages or view them all at once in the dashboard.

Incident History

Local persistence of every state transition. Browse past incidents with timestamps and durations. Auto-purge after 90 days.

Privacy First

No data collected. No analytics. No cloud sync. All monitoring data stays on your device. Connects only to your configured servers.

Battery Aware

Adaptive polling that reduces frequency on battery power. Smart backoff when your Mac is sleeping. Zero impact on battery life.

How it works

Point it at your Kuma instance

No API key. No authentication. Just your status page URL and slug. The app reads the public API endpoints that Uptime Kuma exposes.

Connection
server http://192.168.1.33:3001
slug   home-lab
 
> GET /api/status-page/home-lab
✓ 200 OK — 6 monitors, 2 groups
> GET /api/status-page/heartbeat/home-lab
✓ 200 OK — 144 heartbeats loaded
 
Connection validated. Polling every 30s.
